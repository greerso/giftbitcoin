Quick help

What is this?
Someone gave you real bitcoin. The gift link is what controls it, like cash in an envelope.
Which option should I pick?
Best option: a wallet only you control. Phoenix is a free app that takes a minute to set up. Already have a wallet? Paste its address. An exchange (Coinbase, Kraken) is the simplest fallback, but it holds the bitcoin for you rather than you holding it yourself.
Is this safe?
The link is like cash, so keep it private. This site never holds the bitcoin or your keys.
What if I lose the link?
It can't be recovered by anyone. That's what makes it truly yours. Download the backup when offered.

Recover an unredeemed gift

If your gift card wasn't redeemed before it expired, you can send the bitcoin back to yourself. Paste your gift link or open your backup file.
